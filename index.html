OUR STORY

Education for Life and Future

"Education for Life and Future" is for those who need extra help in stepping the next stage in education. For students who cannot affort tutors or other academies, we are here to help. We want everyone to have the opportunity to boost their knowledge and become the core of this world.

We came up with this idea when we all went to a mission trip in Paterson. Crime rate there was severely high, many families were surviving day by day with milk and bananas. Most importantly, their number one concerns were not being able send their child to College. That's how we came up with this organization.

WHY HELP?

DESPERATE

Why are so many students from poor families still not making it through high school? A lot of it has to do with the rise of high-poverty schools. A high-poverty school is one where more than 75 percent of students are eligible for free or reduced price meals. The most recent data show that one in four public schools in America is a high-poverty school, double what it was back in the 1990s.

WE CAN CHANGE

Going to a school where nearly all of your classmates are poor puts you at a huge disadvantage educationally. Research shows the percentage of a student’s schoolmates who are poor is a strong predictor of academic achievement, even after accounting for a child’s own family income and socioeconomic status.

LIGHT UP THE WORLD

For years, we have beat up on schools with low graduation rates. While that’s been happening, the percentage of high-poverty schools has been growing. There is a lot that great staff and great teaching can do.
